VI. Nyquist stability criterion

If we know Nyquist plot of an open-loop system. We wanna determine stability of the corresponding closed-loop system.

How?

IF W(s) is unstable, then the closed-loop system will be stable if the Nyquist plot of W(jω) encircles at the point (-1,0) as many times, as there are poles of w(s) in the right half plane.
